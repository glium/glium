pub struct StencilCubemapArrayMipmap<'t>(_, _);

Represents a single mipmap level of a StencilCubemapArray.

Can be obtained by calling StencilCubemapArray::mipmap(), StencilCubemapArray::main_level(), StencilCubemapArrayLayer::mipmap() or StencilCubemapArrayLayer::main_level().
