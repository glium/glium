pub struct UnsignedTexture2dMipmap<'t>(_, _);

Represents a single mipmap level of a UnsignedTexture2d.

Can be obtained by calling UnsignedTexture2d::mipmap(), UnsignedTexture2d::main_level(), UnsignedTexture2dLayer::mipmap() or UnsignedTexture2dLayer::main_level().
