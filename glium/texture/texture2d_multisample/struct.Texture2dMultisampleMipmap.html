pub struct Texture2dMultisampleMipmap<'t>(_, _);

Represents a single mipmap level of a Texture2dMultisample.

Can be obtained by calling Texture2dMultisample::mipmap(), Texture2dMultisample::main_level(), Texture2dMultisampleLayer::mipmap() or Texture2dMultisampleLayer::main_level().
